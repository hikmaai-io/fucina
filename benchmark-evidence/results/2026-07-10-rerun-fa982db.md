validation, the first state-cache probe pays one-time setup while the median represents steady
warm behavior.

The exact model ledger remained 23.13 GiB. There were no HTTP 503s, CUDA errors, or allocation
failures. At the largest telemetry window, average batch was 23.1, engine time was 62.80 ms/step,
and non-engine pass overhead remained approximately 0.05 ms/step.
