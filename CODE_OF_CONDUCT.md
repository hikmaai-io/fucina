# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.

In short: be respectful and constructive. Harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language
are not tolerated in issues, pull requests, or any other project space.

The full text is available at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **conduct@hikmaai.io**. Reports are handled confidentially and on a
best-effort basis. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may ban contributors
for behavior they deem inappropriate.
